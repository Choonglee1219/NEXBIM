# Title
Fire resistance of single-layer concrete walls, floors, and roofs

<br><br>

# Content
## 4.2
Concrete bearing or nonbearing walls, floor slabs, and roof slabs required to provide fire-resistance ratings of 1 to 4 hours shall comply with the minimum equivalent thickness values in Table 4.2. For solid walls and slabs with flat surfaces, the equivalent thickness shall be determined in accordance with 4.2.1. The equivalent thickness of hollow-core slabs or walls, or slabs, walls, or other barrier elements with surfaces that are not flat, shall be determined in accordance with 4.2.2 through 4.2.4. Minimum cover protection of steel reinforcement shall be determined in accordance with 4.3.

<br>

| Aggregate type    | 1 hour | 1-1/2 hours | 2 hours | 3 hours | 4 hours |
| ----------------- | :----: | :---------: | :-----: | :-----: | :-----: |
| Siliceous         | 90     | 110         | 125     | 155     | 175     |
| Carbonate         | 80     | 100         | 115     | 145     | 170     |
| Semi-light-weight | 70     | 85          | 95      | 115     | 135     |
| Siliceous         | 65     | 80          | 90      | 110     | 130     |

Table 4.2-Minimum equivalent thickness for fire-resistance rating(mm)
